Our Program

We Support Builders and Entrepreneurs in Our Campus Tech Community

The Builders Incubation Program (BIP) is an 8-week remote summer program that gives students access to the following resources

  • $100 to $5,000 in equity free grants
  • 1-on-1 mentorship from our product, engineering, design, and investor networks
  • Access to connections with VCs and angel investors
  • Weekly workshops and programming
