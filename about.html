Accelerating Harvard Startups

Our mission

Since its inception, HCS has cultivated a community of passionate young technologists and builders, who are hungry to cultivate the next generation of tech. We hope to encourage experimentation and tinkering alongside giving back to the greater Harvard community.

Our vision

Launched in the summer of 2020, the Builders Incubation Program has been able to help 21 teams realize their vision. We hope to give teams the resources and mentorship necessary to transform an idea into a startup.

Frequently asked questions

What are the requirements for applying?

In order to apply for BIP, at least one member of your team must be a current Harvard College student.

What are the program start/end dates?

The program is 7 weeks long and will start June 22 and end on August 8.

How does funding from BIP work?

Students will receive equity free grants that can go towards any project expenses. Common expenses include domain and hosting, data storage, app store fees, or marketing costs. More funding guidelines can be found here.

How do I apply?

Fill out our application form here! Applications are due by June 15, 2021 at 11:59 PST.
